üí° Headlight Safety Tips
  • Use high beams only in rural areas with no oncoming traffic
  • Dim to low beams when within 500 feet of oncoming vehicles
  • Dim to low beams when following within 300 feet of another vehicle
  • Never use high beams in fog - they reflect back and reduce visibility
üåü Glare Reduction Strategies
  • Look Right: Focus on right edge of road when facing bright headlights
  • Clean Windows: Dirty windows scatter light and increase glare
  • Adjust Mirrors: Use night setting on rearview mirror to reduce glare
  • Use Visor: Block overhead streetlights and signs
  • Increase Distance: More space from bright taillights ahead
