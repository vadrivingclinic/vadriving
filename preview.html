üîÑ How The Timer Works

Continuous Operation: Once you start a lesson, the timer begins and runs continuously until you complete it.

Browser Visibility: The timer is visible in your browser window while you're on the lesson page.

Automatic Tracking: Time accumulates automatically and is saved to your progress.

No User Control: You cannot pause, stop, or reset timers - they run continuously.

Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì Can I pause the timer during a lesson?
No. Lesson timers run continuously from start to completion. They cannot be paused, stopped, or reset.
‚ùì What happens if I close my browser during a lesson?
The timer continues running as long as the lesson page is open. If you close the browser, you'll need to restart the lesson to continue timing.
‚ùì Can I complete the course faster than 8 hours?
No. Virginia DMV requires exactly 8 hours of instructional time. Each lesson has minimum time requirements that must be met.
‚ùì How do I know how much time I have left?
Your student dashboard shows real-time progress, including total hours completed and hours remaining to reach 8 hours.
‚ùì Where is the timer displayed?
The timer is visible in your browser window while you're on a lesson page. It shows the elapsed time for that specific lesson.
‚ùì Can I see my total accumulated time?
Yes. Your student dashboard shows your total accumulated time across all completed lessons.
