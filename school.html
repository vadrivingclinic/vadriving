‚è∞ Waiting for the Bus Safely

‚è∞
Arrival & Positioning
  • Arrive 5 minutes before scheduled time
  • Wait in safe area away from traffic
  • Stand 3 giant steps (6 feet) from curb
  • No playing, running, or pushing
  • Wait for complete bus stop
üë£
Boarding & Exiting
  • Wait for driver's signal to approach
  • Use handrails - always, every time
  • No pushing or rushing on stairs
  • Move immediately away from bus
  • Stay in driver's line of sight
üö∂
Street Crossing Protocol
  • WAIT for driver's hand signal
  • Cross ONLY in front of bus
  • Make eye contact with driver
  • Look left-right-left before stepping
  • Walk, don't run, across street

üöå On the Bus Behavior

Remain seated facing forward at all times
Keep aisle clear of bags and belongings
Speak quietly - no yelling or screaming
Keep head, hands, and arms inside bus
Follow driver's instructions immediately
No eating, drinking, or gum chewing
Respect other students and property
Know emergency exit locations

üìç Understanding the Danger Zone

DANGER ZONE: 10 feet around bus
Safe area: Outside red zone
üéØ Danger Zone Safety Rules
  • NEVER walk close to bus (front, sides, or back)
  • If you drop something near bus, LEAVE IT - tell driver
  • Always cross in FRONT of bus where driver can see you
  • Make eye contact with driver before crossing
  • If driver can't see you, you're in the danger zone
  • When getting off, move immediately away from bus
