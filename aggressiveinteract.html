üõ°Ô∏è Prevention Strategies & Safe Responses

üõ°Ô∏è Your Defensive Driving Toolkit
  • Stay Calm: Practice deep breathing when frustrated. Count to ten before reacting.
  • Plan Ahead: Leave 10-15 minutes early to avoid time pressure.
  • Safe Following Distance: Maintain 3-4 seconds between you and the car ahead.
  • Don't Engage: Never make eye contact or gesture at aggressive drivers.
  • Move Over: Safely change lanes or exit to let aggressive drivers pass.
  • Report Safely: Note license plates and call 911 if behavior is dangerous.
