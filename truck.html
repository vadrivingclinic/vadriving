üöó Advice for Passenger Vehicle Drivers
  • If you can't see the driver in their mirror, they can't see you
  • Never linger beside a truck, especially on the right side
  • When passing, do so quickly and completely
  • Don't cut off trucks - they need 2-3 football fields to stop
  • Watch for truck turn signals well in advance
  • Give trucks extra space at intersections
  • Be patient - trucks accelerate much slower than cars
