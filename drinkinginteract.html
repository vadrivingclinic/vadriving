üõ°Ô∏è Prevention Strategies & Solutions

üì± Plan Ahead for Safety

The most effective way to prevent drunk driving is to plan your transportation BEFORE you start drinking. Making decisions about getting home should happen when you're still sober.

‚úÖ Your Prevention Toolkit
  • Designated Driver: Always have a sober driver in your group
  • Ride-Sharing: Use Uber, Lyft, or traditional taxi services
  • Public Transit: Plan your route using buses or trains
  • Stay Overnight: If you can't get home safely, stay where you are
  • Technology: Use apps that help prevent drunk driving

üë• Be a Responsible Friend

If you see a friend about to drive impaired, it's your responsibility to intervene. You could be saving their life and the lives of others on the road.

ü§ù How to Help a Friend
  • Offer to call them a ride
  • Take their keys away if necessary
  • Provide a place to sleep
  • Never let an impaired friend drive alone
  • Educate others about the dangers
